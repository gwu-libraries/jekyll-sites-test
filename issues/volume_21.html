Global Business Languages publishes original, peer-reviewed articles that make a significant contribution to the field of Business Language Studies. Embracing a broad definition of “business,” we welcome submissions in a variety of related interdisciplinary subfields within Language for Specific Purposes, such as language for the arts, law, healthcare, engineering, etc.
